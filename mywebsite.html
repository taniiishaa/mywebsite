Empowering Tribal Communities Through Forest Rights Act Implementation

A comprehensive platform for digitizing, managing, and visualizing FRA data with AI-powered insights and decision support systems.

Key Features & Capabilities

Data Digitization

AI-powered extraction and standardization of legacy FRA records with NER for automated data processing.

FRA Atlas

Interactive WebGIS portal visualizing FRA claims, granted titles, and forest resources in real-time.

AI Asset Mapping

Computer vision and ML algorithms to map agricultural land, water bodies, and forest resources from satellite imagery.

Decision Support System

AI-enhanced DSS for scheme layering and policy recommendations based on integrated data analysis.

Remote Sensing Integration

Satellite-based monitoring of forest cover, land use changes, and natural resources in FRA areas.

Stakeholder Collaboration

Multi-departmental platform for Tribal Affairs, Forest Department, and other line departments.
